Review UI/UX design for visual hierarchy, interaction patterns, design system consistency, usability heuristics, motion, and accessibility.

Focus on: visual hierarchy and layout, interaction feedback (loading/error/empty states), design token consistency, Nielsen's heuristics, responsive behavior, micro-interactions, color contrast, and Figma-to-code parity.
